About the Workshop

Three Days of
Deep Learning

This national workshop covers the entire computational metagenomics pipeline — from raw sequencing data to biological insights. You'll work hands-on with real datasets and the exact tools used in published research.

Hosted by the Wadhwani School of Data Science & AI (WSAI) and delivered through NPTEL+, this is one of India's few intensive, in-person workshops on metagenomic data analysis.

  • Amplicon metagenomics & 16S rRNA analysis
  • Shotgun sequencing, assembly, and functional annotation
  • Statistical analysis, ML in microbiome research
  • Hands-on practicals every day with real datasets
  • Certificate of participation provided

the Experts

Prof. Karthik Raman
Professor, Dept. of Data Science & AI · Wadhwani School of DS & AI (WSAI), IIT Madras

Prof. Karthik Raman leads the Raman Lab at IIT Madras, working at the crossroads of computational biology, systems biology, and machine learning. His research spans metabolic network modelling, microbiome data analysis, and ML-driven biological discovery — with numerous high-impact publications and open-source tools widely adopted by the research community.
